Paneer Butter Masala

A creamy and rich Indian curry made with paneer, tomatoes, and aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • 200g Paneer (cottage cheese)
  • 2 medium Onions
  • 3 medium Tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp Butter
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic paste
  • 1 tsp Red chili powder
  • 1 tsp Garam masala
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a pan and saut√© onions until golden.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add tomatoes and spices, cook until soft.
  4. Blend into a smooth paste and return to the pan.
  5. Add paneer cubes, simmer for 5 minutes.
  6. Serve hot with naan or rice.
